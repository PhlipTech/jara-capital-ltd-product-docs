7. Outbound payments – retries and audit

Invest, withdraw, and wallet mutations initiated by your API: correlation ID per request, idempotency keys, append-only audit. Retries only for transient provider or network errors, exponential backoff with jitter, cap and dead-letter.

flowchart TD R[Client request invest withdraw wallet op] --> V[Validate auth limits idempotency key] V --> A1[Audit PAYMENT_REQUEST_RECEIVED] A1 --> P[Call Cowrywise with idempotency header] P --> OUT{Outcome} OUT -->|2xx success| A2[Audit PROVIDER_SUCCESS] A2 --> S[Persist wallet or txn state] S --> A3[Audit STATE_UPDATED] A3 --> OK[Return success to client] OUT -->|4xx permanent| A4[Audit PROVIDER_REJECTED] A4 --> UERR[Return error to user] OUT -->|5xx or timeout| A5[Audit PROVIDER_TRANSIENT] A5 --> RET{Retries below max?} RET -->|Yes| A6[Audit RETRY_SCHEDULED] A6 --> QJ[Enqueue job backoff plus jitter] QJ --> WK[Worker executes later] WK --> P RET -->|No| A7[Audit PAYMENT_FAILED_EXHAUSTED] A7 --> DLQ[Dead letter queue plus alert] A7 --> UMSG[User sees clear message plus support]

8. Inbound webhooks – verification and audit

PhlipVerify and Cowrywise (and bank rails) may POST status events. Verify signatures, dedupe by event id, apply once, log every step for dispute and compliance.

flowchart TD H[HTTPS POST webhook] --> S1[Verify signature and timestamp] S1 -->|invalid| A0[Audit WEBHOOK_REJECTED] A0 --> R401[Return 401] S1 -->|ok| A1[Audit WEBHOOK_RECEIVED] A1 --> DED{Event id seen before?} DED -->|Yes| A2[Audit WEBHOOK_DUPLICATE_SKIP] A2 --> R200[Return 200 idempotent] DED -->|No| PROC[Apply business update balance or status] PROC --> A3[Audit WEBHOOK_APPLIED] A3 --> PERS[Persist idempotency row for event id] PERS --> A4[Audit STATE_UPDATED] A4 --> R200B[Return 200]

9. Auto-Save rule lifecycle (phased)

User-defined recurring or rule-based moves into savings/wallet. Scheduler runs on your backend or partner; every execution is idempotent and audited.

flowchart TD U[User creates or edits rule] --> C[Consent and disclosure] C --> S[Save rule in app DB] S --> A1[Audit AUTOSAVE_RULE_CREATED] A1 --> SYNC{Partner recurring or mandate API?} SYNC -->|Yes| P[Register schedule with partner] P --> A2[Audit AUTOSAVE_PARTNER_REGISTERED] SYNC -->|No| Q2[Enqueue local scheduler job] Q2 --> A3[Audit AUTOSAVE_SCHEDULED_LOCAL] A2 --> T[Tick due execution] A3 --> T T --> I[Idempotency check for run id] I --> A4[Audit AUTOSAVE_ATTEMPT] A4 --> X[Execute transfer or debit via partner] X --> OK{Success?} OK -->|Yes| A5[Audit AUTOSAVE_SUCCESS] OK -->|No| A6[Audit AUTOSAVE_FAIL] A6 --> R[Retry policy or user notify]
